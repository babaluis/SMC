How to Keep Teenagers Safe

1. Teach your kids not to share personal information on social media

Educate your children about what information shouldn’t be shared on social media.
For example, teach them never to post their:

  • Home address
  • Full, legal name
  • Online passwords
  • School name or address
  • Date of birth
  • Any banking or credit card information

2. Use parental controls to block harmful content

3. Set time limits for your kids’ screen time

4. Empower kids to use blocking and reporting tools

5. Teach kids not to click on or download unknown links and files

6. Disable location sharing, and ask teens not to geo-tag photos
